"Managing AFS"

February 2020

A little over 20 years ago, I wrote the book "Managing AFS: The Andrew File System" for Prentice-Hall Publishing. Late last year, a random article on Reddit mentioned that authors could ask their publisher for a "Revocation of Rights" to transfer copyright back to the author. I asked, and Prentice-Hall graciously agreed.

So, now, here are the original pages of the book, along with reformatting for the web. I'll also add the small backstory and some random comments on AFS and the state of distributed computing (a.k.a. the cloud).

Richard Campbell
