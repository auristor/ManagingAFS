COSI / SaaS CITI / Misc UNIX / X11 IFS group / Mainframe vs NFS About 1989/90 asked O'Reilly about an AFS book. Not terribly interested; wanted a DCE/DFS book. One response, "wonder if you've backed the wrong horse? NFS?" So what? only one programming language ? Get to Morgan Stanley AFS; obvious advantages Go to UBS for DCE/DFS Springer Verlag responds; sounds great. Mention it to colleague, wife works for Prentice-Hall; contact, contract. 1995 Devote 3 nights a week, three hours a night. After year and a half, 4 nights. Revisions illustrations Not much support; not enough outreach on my part Some said, make it a small book. How to do that? It's a rats nest. And who/when will other books be written?? Best part, I think, is that I was able to write a story over many chapters that made sense. Illustrations major fail. One more massive editorial rewrite over 24 hours.
